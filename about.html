AI is amazing, but everyone everywhere is calling themselves AI. It's tough to know who can help with what.

Nyckel isn't trying to be everything to everyone. Instead, we're hyper-focused on one powerful AI use case: image and text classification. Our vision is to make it as easy as possible to auto-label content at scale.
